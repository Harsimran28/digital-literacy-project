## Part B – Google Workspace Collaboration (Google Forms)

### Platform Used
Google Forms (forms.google.com) + Google Sheets (for responses)

### What I Built
I created a 5-question Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz titled
**"Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz"** designed for first-year students at
VIT Bhopal University. The quiz covers:

1. What digital literacy means (MCQ)
2. Safe internet practices (MCQ)
3. How to handle phishing emails (MCQ)
4. Best platform for showcasing coding projects (MCQ)
5. One personal step to improve digital safety (Short Answer)

The form is linked to a Google Sheet that automatically records all responses,
making it easy to track how well batchmates understand digital literacy concepts.

## How These Tools Will Help Me Academically

HackerRank will be an important part of my academic journey as a CSE student.
Most technical interviews and campus placements involve coding challenges on
platforms exactly like this one. Starting early — even with simple problems —
builds the habit of thinking algorithmically and reading problem statements carefully.

Google Forms and Google Sheets together are incredibly useful for collaboration.
As a student, I can use Forms for peer feedback, project surveys, quiz creation,
and data collection for assignments. The automatic response tracking in Google
Sheets saves time and makes analysis straightforward. These are tools I will
realistically use throughout all four years of my degree, not just for this project.
