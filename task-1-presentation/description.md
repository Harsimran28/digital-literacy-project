# Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

## Tool Used
Designed using HTML/CSS with browser-based rendering and exported as a PDF.

## What the Infographic Shows
The infographic covers three core topics:
1. **What is Digital Literacy** – A definition explaining that digital literacy goes beyond just using devices; it includes evaluating online information, protecting personal data, and communicating professionally.
2. **Safe Internet Practices** – Six practical tips for students including using strong passwords, never sharing OTPs, checking URLs before entering details, and enabling two-factor authentication.
3. **Professional Online Presence** – Tips on building a LinkedIn and GitHub profile, maintaining a clean digital footprint, and how online profiles impact internship and job opportunities.
4. **Email Etiquette Essentials** – Key rules for writing professional emails including subject lines, greetings, and sign-offs.

The design uses a dark navy and cyan colour palette with a grid background to give it a clean, tech-focused aesthetic suitable for a CSE student audience.

## Key Statistics Highlighted
- 5 Billion+ internet users worldwide
- 80% of employers check online profiles before hiring
- 3.4 Billion phishing emails sent every single day
